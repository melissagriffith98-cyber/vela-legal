Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Vela is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you use the Vela app.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Information you provide directly:

  • Name, email address, and username when you create an account
  • Skin profile information including skin type, skin tone, and skin concerns
  • Beauty aesthetic preferences selected during onboarding
  • Posts, captions, and photos you share in Vela's Circle
  • Products you save to your Vanity and routines you build

Information collected automatically:

  • How you interact with the app including products you swipe, save, or pass
  • Device information including device type and operating system
  • App performance data and crash reports

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • Provide and personalize the Vela experience based on your skin profile
  • Recommend products that match your skin type, concerns, and aesthetic preferences
  • Enable community features including Vela's Circle and public profiles
  • Improve our product database, recommendation algorithm, and app features
  • Communicate with you about app updates and important notices
  • Ensure the security and integrity of the platform

3. SKIN PROFILE DATA

We treat your skin profile information with extra care. This data is:

  • Used solely to personalize your product recommendations within Vela
  • Never sold to brands, advertisers, or third parties
  • Never shared without your explicit consent
  • Not used to build advertising profiles
  • Stored securely with access controls

4. USER CONTENT AND PUBLIC INFORMATION

Content you post publicly in Vela's Circle — including captions, photos, and tagged products — is visible to other Vela users. Your public profile, including your username, skin profile tags, Almost Mine products, routines, and Top 5, may be viewed by other users. We do not use your photos for facial recognition or biometric identification.

5. INFORMATION SHARING

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: We work with trusted third-party providers including Supabase (database infrastructure). These providers only access your data to perform services on our behalf.
  • Affiliate programs: When you click a product link and make a purchase, the retailer may receive standard affiliate tracking data. This does not include your name or personal profile.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request.

6. AFFILIATE DISCLOSURE

Vela participates in affiliate marketing programs with beauty retailers. When you purchase products through links in our app, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. This never influences our product recommendations.

7. DATA SECURITY

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted data transmission and secure database storage. No method of transmission is 100% secure.

8. DATA RETENTION

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we will delete your personal information within 30 days.

9. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Vela is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has used Vela, please contact us at privacy@velaapp.io.

10. YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt-out: Opt out of certain data uses including marketing communications

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA. We do not sell personal information. To exercise any rights, contact us at privacy@velaapp.io.

11. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email.
